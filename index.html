What's built today

A real app, not a mockup.

TacoSauce is in active development. Here's what operators can use right now.

🗓️

Event calendar

Monthly view with FullCalendar. Click any day to add an event. Event types: market, catering, private, blocked.

🔁

Recurring events

Set up weekly, biweekly, or monthly recurring events with an optional end date. Perfect for regular markets.

📋

Prep list templates

Save reusable prep lists in Settings. Load them into any event with one tap, or save directly from an event.

💰

Revenue targets

Set a revenue goal per event. Shows up in your calendar data so you can track actuals against targets later.

🔗

Google Calendar sync

Connect once — events push automatically to a dedicated TacoSauce calendar in your Google account.

🍎

iCal / Apple Calendar

Subscribe in Apple Calendar, Outlook, or any iCal app. One-tap "Add to Apple Calendar" button in Settings.

Operator Tools

Quick calculators for food truck decisions.

All instant, no sign-in needed. Sliders update results in real time.

📍
Event go/no-go Enter attendance, truck count, avg spend, food cost % → instant verdict with estimated profit and margin.
🌮
Menu pricing Enter ingredient cost and target food cost % → minimum sell price, suggested price (rounded to nearest $0.50), and multiplier.
🍽️
Catering quote Headcount × per-person cost + overhead → quote range based on 25–30% food cost. Per-person breakdown included.
💰
Break-even Monthly fixed costs ÷ gross margin → events per month needed to cover your nut. Days per week included.
